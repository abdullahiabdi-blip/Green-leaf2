Plant Care Guides

Monstera

Light: Bright, indirect

Water: Weekly, let soil dry

Tip: Wipe leaves with a damp cloth.

Snake Plant

Light: Low to bright

Water: Every 2-3 weeks

Tip: Very hardy, perfect for beginners.

Pothos

Light: Low to bright indirect

Water: Weekly, let soil dry

Tip: Tolerates low light well.

Fiddle Leaf Fig

Light: Bright, indirect

Water: Weekly

Tip: Keep away from drafts.

Spider Plant

Light: Bright indirect

Water: Weekly

Tip: Pet-friendly and easy to grow.

ZZ Plant

Light: Low light

Water: Every 2-3 weeks

Tip: Very low maintenance.

Aloe Vera

Light: Bright direct

Water: Every 2-3 weeks

Tip: Great for healing and skincare.

String of Pearls

Light: Bright indirect

Water: When soil is dry

Tip: Allow soil to dry between watering.

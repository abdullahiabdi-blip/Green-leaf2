About GreenLeaf

Our Story

GreenLeaf was born in 2020 from a simple idea: every home deserves a touch of green. What started as a small balcony garden in Nairobi has grown into a thriving plant shop serving plant lovers across Kenya.

We believe that plants are more than just decoration — they bring joy, peace, and wellness to our lives. Our mission is to make it easy for everyone to experience the magic of indoor gardening.

Every plant we sell is carefully sourced from sustainable growers who share our values. We inspect each plant before shipping to ensure it arrives healthy and ready to thrive in your home.
